The Problem Ballot On Chain solves
1. Ballot On Chain revolutionizes the way elections are conducted. Since last 250 odd years, elections are conducted using paper ballot which serves the purpose of authenticity and secrecy as person needs to be physically present to cast their vote.

2. Ballet On Chain solves this problem of conducting elections on such large scale in democracies like India & USA where elections take months to conclude at times due to sheer population size.

3. Ballot On Chain uses the blockchain to bring voting power on your fingertips saving time money efforts and productivity losses which goes into conducting the physical elections.

4. Ballot on Chain addresses the issues of authenticity of voter who votes remotely as we use two factor authentication and use registered mobile number, and secrecy of the ballot sacrosanct.
